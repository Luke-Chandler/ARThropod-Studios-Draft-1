My work
I generalize in a variety 2D and 3D designs within art and animation.  Each new project, either personal or professional starts with a narrative.  To me, everything must have story in one way or another for me to realise it visually.
My skills
As an artist in general, I started from 2D sketching, and my skills revolved around drawing characters. I strengthened these skills throughout my education and branched out during my extended diploma course. This is where I began studying into areas of digital painting, print, editing, 3D design and animation.
My 3D work
To me, the process of 3D modelling is essentially similar to sketching. There is no one way of creating something on a piece of 3D software. This applies to characters, environments and objects. I use Autodesk Maya and Mudbox to produce all my models, rigs, textures and animations. Please support Autodesk for all their amazing software.
